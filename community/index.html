Safety

Community Guidelines

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Casting Quarter brings artists and casting professionals together. These guidelines explain the behaviour expected from everyone using the platform.

1. Be Honest

Use accurate account, profile, company, casting and application information. Do not impersonate another person or organisation, create deceptive accounts, or misrepresent experience, work, payment or opportunities.

2. Keep Opportunities Genuine

Companies must only publish genuine casting opportunities they are authorised to offer. Casting information, dates, locations, requirements and payment details must be clear and truthful.

3. Communicate Professionally

Keep applications and messages relevant, respectful and professional. Harassment, threats, hate, discrimination, bullying, sexual exploitation, unwanted sexual content and abusive behaviour are not allowed.

4. Protect People From Scams And Exploitation

Do not use Casting Quarter for fraud, phishing, spam, financial scams, unsafe requests or illegal activity. Companies must not request money from performers through casting messages or misrepresent fees, employment or payment.

5. Respect Privacy And Content

Only upload or share information, photos, videos, CVs and other media that you have permission to use. Do not copy, download, publish or misuse another user's personal information or media.

6. Use Safety Tools Responsibly

Report suspicious, unsafe or inappropriate profiles, castings and messages. Do not knowingly submit false or malicious reports. Casting Quarter may review reported activity and request further information.

7. Follow Platform Decisions

Do not attempt to bypass account verification, subscriptions, privacy settings, messaging restrictions, moderation actions or other platform security controls.

8. Enforcement

Content may be hidden or removed while concerns are reviewed. Casting Quarter may warn users, restrict features, lock conversations, suspend accounts or remove accounts when these guidelines or the Platform Terms are broken.

9. Get Help

For safety concerns or questions about these guidelines, use the reporting tools in the app or email support@castingquarter.com.
