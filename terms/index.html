Platform Terms

Last updated: 15 July 2026

These Platform Terms explain the basic rules for using Casting Quarter.

1. About Casting Quarter

Casting Quarter is a casting platform that helps artists and performers discover casting opportunities and helps companies, agencies and casting teams find suitable talent.

2. Accounts

Users must provide accurate information when creating an account.

Artists are responsible for keeping their profile, media, CV, skills, availability and application details truthful and up to date.

Companies are responsible for keeping their company profile, casting posts, contact information and project information truthful and up to date.

Users must not create fake accounts, impersonate another person or company, or use someone else's content without permission.

3. Company Verification

Company accounts may require moderator approval before they can fully use Casting Quarter.

Casting Quarter may request additional information to verify a company profile. Approval may be refused, delayed, suspended or removed if the information is incomplete, misleading or unsafe.

4. Castings

Companies must only post genuine casting opportunities.

Casting posts must not be misleading, discriminatory, fraudulent, unsafe, illegal or inappropriate.

Casting Quarter may hide, edit, suspend or remove casting posts that are reported or appear to break platform rules.

5. Artist Profiles And Media

Artists must only upload photos, videos, CVs, showreels and profile information that they have permission to use.

Artists must not upload illegal, abusive, sexual, exploitative, hateful, fraudulent or misleading content.

Casting Quarter may hide, suspend or remove artist profiles or media that are reported or appear to break platform rules.

6. Applications And Shortlists

Artists can apply for castings where the app allows it.

Companies can review applications, shortlist applicants and remove applicants from shortlist where appropriate.

Shortlisting does not guarantee employment, engagement, payment or selection.

7. Messaging

Proposal messaging is available only where the app rules allow it, including account status, shortlist status, privacy settings and subscription status.

Users must keep messages professional and safe.

Users must not use messages for harassment, spam, scams, impersonation, abusive content, illegal activity or unsafe requests.

Casting Quarter may moderate, restrict or lock messaging where required for safety, account status, application status or platform misuse.

8. Subscriptions

Some Casting Quarter features require an active subscription.

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple manages billing, renewal, cancellation and refunds according to Apple's rules.

If a subscription expires or is cancelled, premium features may stop working.

9. Reports And Moderation

Users can report castings, profiles, messages or safety concerns.

Casting Quarter may review reports and take action, including requesting more information, warning users, hiding content, locking messages, suspending accounts, removing content or closing reports.

Casting Quarter may not be able to investigate every report immediately.

10. Safety

Users are responsible for making their own professional and personal safety decisions when communicating or working with others.

Casting Quarter does not guarantee that every artist, company, casting or message is accurate, safe or suitable.

Users should report suspicious, unsafe or inappropriate activity.

11. Prohibited Use

Users must not:

  • break the law;
  • post fake, misleading or fraudulent opportunities;
  • impersonate another person or organisation;
  • upload content they do not own or have permission to use;
  • harass, threaten, abuse or discriminate against others;
  • send spam or scams;
  • attempt to bypass app security, payments, verification or moderation;
  • scrape, copy or misuse profile, media, casting or message data.

12. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion in the app. Deletion removes account access and account data where deletion is legally and technically possible.

Some records may be retained where required for legal, safety, fraud-prevention, accounting, dispute or moderation reasons.

13. Changes To Casting Quarter

Casting Quarter may update, change, pause or remove features as the platform develops.

14. Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to support@castingquarter.com.
