Subscriptions

Casting Quarter offers Artist Premium and Company Premium as monthly, three-month and annual auto-renewable subscriptions.

PricingAvailable plans, current local prices and included features are displayed in the app before purchase.
BillingPayment is charged to your Apple Account after confirmation.
Auto-renewalSubscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
Manage anytimeManage or cancel your subscription through your Apple Account settings. Existing purchases can be restored within the app.
